# Personal Wiki Ideas

## Source Summary
Notes on personal wiki building, research directions, tools, and pain points for maintaining a personal knowledge base. Outlines goals for a self-improving wiki system.

## Key Concepts
- Self-compiling wiki from raw materials
- LLM-assisted note taking
- Second brain systems
- Knowledge organization

## Key Takeaways
1. Build a wiki that compiles itself from raw materials
2. The wiki should improve its own structure over time
3. Pain points: keeping wiki in sync, too many "later" items, difficulty finding things

## Research Directions
- Encoding "taste" in a model
- Cross-domain analogy detection
- Synthesizing data from multiple sources
